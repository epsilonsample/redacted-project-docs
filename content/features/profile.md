# Profile Settings

The Profile page allows you to configure your user preferences, change roles (if applicable), and set your active working location.

!!! info "Availability"
    All roles have access to Profile settings.

## Accessing Profile

1. Click the **â˜°** (hamburger) menu icon at the top left.
2. Select the **Profile** option from the dropdown.

## Profile Options

### Mobile Number

Add or update your mobile phone number for notifications.

1. Enter your number in the **Mobile Number** field.
2. Click the **OK** button to save.

### Changing Roles { #changing-roles }

If you have access to multiple roles, you can switch between them:

1. Click the **Role** dropdown.
2. Select your desired role from the list.
3. Click the **OK** button to save.

![Role Selection Placeholder](../assets/images/placeholder-role-dropdown.svg)

!!! note "Role-Specific Home Pages"
    After changing your role, you'll be redirected to that role's home page.

### Setting Your Location

Configure your active working location:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Active Company** | Displays your company (typically not editable) |
| **Active District** | Select your working district |
| **Active Location** | Select your specific location |
| **Stockpoint** | Select your inventory stockpoint |

!!! warning "Save Your Changes"
    You must click **OK** to save any profile changes. Navigating away without clicking OK will discard your changes.

## Steps to Update Profile

1. Navigate to the **Profile** view from the left menu.
2. Make your desired changes:
    - Update mobile number
    - Select a different role.
    - Change district, location, or stockpoint.
3. Click **OK** at the top or bottom of the page.
4. You'll be redirected to the appropriate home page.
