# How to Accept Bits That Were Sent To You

When another user transfers bits to you, you'll need to accept them before they appear in your inventory.

## Prerequisites

- Bits have been sent to you via eTransfer.
- You have access to the eTransfer Tickets view.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **eTransfer Tickets** from the left menu.
2. Look in the **Received eTransfers** section.
3. Ensure the toggle is set to **Pending**.
4. Find the transfer you want to accept.
5. Click on the transfer row to open details.
6. Review the transfer information.
7. Click **Accept** to accept the bits.

## After Accepting

- The bits will appear in your inventory.
- The transfer status changes to "Received".
- The sender is notified of the acceptance.

## If You Need to Reject

If the transfer was sent in error:

1. Click on the transfer row.
2. Click **Reject** instead of Accept.
3. The bits return to the sender.
