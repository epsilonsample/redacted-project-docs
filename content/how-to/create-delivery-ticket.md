# How to Create a Delivery Ticket

Create a delivery ticket to document bit delivery to a customer.

## Prerequisites

- Field Sales role
- Access to My Bits view
- Bit is in your inventory.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **My Bits**.
2. Find the bit you want to deliver.
3. Click on the bit row to open Bit Details.
4. Click **Create Delivery Ticket**.
5. Enter the delivery information:
   - Customer name
   - Well name and location
   - Delivery date
   - Any special notes
6. Review all information
7. Click **Submit**

## Required Information

A delivery ticket typically includes:

- Bit serial number (auto-populated)
- Customer details
- Well information
- Delivery date and time
- Salesperson (auto-populated)

## After Creating

- The delivery ticket is created.
- The bit moves to the eBilling Workbench.
- You can add additional bits to the ticket if needed.
- Proceed with service receipt creation when ready.

## Adding Tariff Surcharge

If a tariff surcharge applies:

1. Look for the **Tariff Surcharge** toggle on the delivery ticket.
2. Enable the toggle to add the surcharge.
3. The surcharge is included in the billing documentation.
