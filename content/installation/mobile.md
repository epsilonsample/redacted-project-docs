# Mobile/iPad Installation

Install the application on your iOS mobile device or iPad.

## Prerequisites

- iOS device (iPhone or iPad)
- Intune Company Portal app installed.
- Network credentials

## Step 1: Install Intune Company Portal

If you haven't already installed the Company Portal:

1. Open the **App Store**.
2. Search for **Intune Company Portal**.
3. Download and install the app.
4. Open Company Portal and tap **Begin**.
5. Follow the steps to set up access.
6. Complete device enrollment

## Step 2: Install the Application

1. Open the **Comp Portal** app.
2. Search for the application (UAT or Production).
3. Tap on the application.
4. Tap the **Install** button.
5. Wait approximately 5 minutes for installation.

!!! note "Already Installed?"
    If the button says **Re-install**, the app is already on your device.

## Step 3: First-Time Sign In

1. Locate the app icon on your home screen.
2. Tap to open.
3. Tap **Continue** on the first screen.
4. Tap **Continue** again to access the application.

### Enter Credentials

1. Enter your email address.
2. Tap **Next**.
3. Enter your password on your organization's sign-in page.
4. Tap **Sign In**.

### Verify Identity

1. Choose your verification method.
2. Complete MFA verification.
3. Choose **Yes** to stay signed in.

### Complete Setup

1. Allow or deny notifications (your choice).
2. Tap **Log In**.
3. Configure your profile settings.

!!! success "Installation Complete"
    You're now ready to use the application on your mobile device.

## Version Selection

| Version | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **UAT** | Testing environment |
| **Production** | Live business use |

!!! warning
    Always use **Production** for daily work. UAT is for testing only.

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Found in Company Portal

Contact IT Support to request access.

### Installation Takes Too Long

- Check your internet connection.
- Try closing and reopening Company Portal.
- Contact HelpDesk if the issue persists.

### Sign-In Issues

- Verify your network credentials.
- Ensure MFA is configured.
- Contact HelpDesk for any account issues.

## Notes

The first-time sign-in process only occurs:

- On initial installation.
- When you change your password.
