# Bit Details View

The Bit Details view shows complete information about a specific bit.

## Accessing Bit Details

Click on any bit row in these views to open Bit Details:

- Inventory Management
- My Bits
- Find Bits

## Information Displayed

### Bit Information

- Serial number
- Product group and type
- Bit size
- Current status
- Current location

### Location History

- Where the bit has been
- Transfer dates
- Previous holders

### Actions Available

Depending on your role, you may see:

- **Send** — Transfer the bit to another user
- **Quick Send** — Rapid transfer
- **Lock/Unlock** — Prevent or allow transfers
- **Consign** — Place with a customer
- **Create Delivery Ticket** — Document a delivery
- **Add to Existing Ticket** — Add to an open ticket
